The Power of Specialization

De-coupling routes creates two highly efficient roles from one inefficient one.

The Newspaper Specialist

Focused on rapid, predictable distribution. No parcel delays means faster routes and the ability to cover more ground in less time.

The Parcel Specialist

An expert in nighttime navigation and efficient locker drops. This role maximizes throughput to achieve peak productivity.

Proof Point: Posten Norge successfully implemented this approach, reducing their total delivery routes from 5,000 to just 2,800 through specialization.
